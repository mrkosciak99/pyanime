One of many

PyAnime is simply a hand-coded Python application for organizing the anime titles you've watched. Why “one of many”? Just type “How to make your own anime list” into any browser, and you'll instantly see major websites pop up. This, however, is just the app's website—not a massive database of titles with hundreds of employees—but something created in my spare time, in the comfort of my free time.

What is it?

  • Source code

    A simple and straightforward app developed on the fly in Python. No hidden features—just what you see.

  • Documentation

    All project documentation and source code are available on GitHub.

  • Completely free

    I take my time and work at my own pace, without any imposed budget or big brands.

What now?

Constantly being developed and enhanced.
Go to GitHub, download it, and give it a try—you might just switch to the app.
